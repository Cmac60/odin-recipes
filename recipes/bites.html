Churro Bites

Description:

Churro bites are a favorite at Taco Bell, but are relatively easy to make at home as well.

All you need are some basic utensils, an oven or preferrably air-fryer, a bit of time and the following list of ingredients!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 angel food cake, cubed
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened

Steps to make:

  1. Place cubed angel food bites in air-fryer/oven, spray with oil and cook at 350 for 5 minutes.
  2. Combine cinnamon and sugar together, pour over bites. Pour bites and sugar into a bag and shake until bites are coated.
  3. Use mixing bowl to combine cream cheese, milk and butter into dipping sauce and mix until smooth. Add sugar to thicken or milk to thin.
