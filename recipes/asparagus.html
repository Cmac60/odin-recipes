Asparagus and Eggs

Description:

Having tried these two foods individually, I have no doubt that they would go great together! The healthy addition to breakfast is a nice plus.

This is one where most of the ingredients are optional, to be honest. They simply bring out the flavor palette well.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, finely chopped
  • 8 thin-stemmed asparagus stalks, trimmed
  • 2 large eggs
  • salt and fresh ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 pinch red pepper flakes (optional)

Steps to make:

  1. Heat small skillet over medium-high heat. Add olive oil and garlic, cook and stir until garlic is fragrant, ~30 seconds.
  2. Add asparagus and cook until fork-tender, turning often to ensure even cooking, ~4 minutes.
  3. Spread asparagus out and crack eggs over it. Cover with lid and cook until egg whites are firm and yolk is done to desired consistency, ~4 minutes.
  4. Season with salt, black pepper and red pepper.
