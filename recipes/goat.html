Baked Goat Cheese Dip

Description:

I've never tried this recipe, but it looks simple, easy and delicious!

The only difficult ingredient is the cheese itself, simply due to cost.

Ingredients:

  • 1 (8oz) goat cheese log
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher or sea salt (optional)
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, cut into ribbons

Steps to make:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Press goat cheese to fit shape of a small baking dish, leaving 1-inch border around the edge. Spoon marinara around border, covering the edge of the cheese but not the middle. Salt to taste.
  3. Bake until cheese is hot and soft and marinara hot and bubbling, 20 to 25 minutes.
  4. Remove from oven, sprinkle with fresh chopped basil.
